After "Project Hail Mary", my reading inspiration is stuck. I don't know, maybe I need some new interesting books. Or my English reading skill is just declining :)

## 7. Đêm Trắng - Fyodor Dostoevsky

This book I bought from Đông Tây Publishing on GG Books; sadly, it's `.pdf` instead of `.epub`. I wrote messages to them, hopefully they can improve in the near future.

I have read this work in English. I have to say translating it into Vietnamese isn't easy. I am still confused when reading it; the characters are sophisticated. Maybe in the end, it's a mere dream for the character.

Rate: 4/5 (if I remember correctly)

## 8. Xứ Tuyết - Kawabata Yasunari

Lol, I am not impressed by this book. Its words and scenery descriptions are wonderful, aesthetic with a melancholic beauty, but the story sucks in my view. There are some plot holes that make me unable to enjoy the work.

Rate: 2/5
