## My plan

First, my very big problem that I am very busy with classes and my Mathematics self-study schedule. So it took me some days to really dig into the ideas and have steps to make it come true.

I divided my plan into 4 stages:

### Stage 1: Statistics, Python and Data Structures

- **Statistics**: I will learn from the book [Practical Statistics for Data Scientists, 2nd Edition](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/practical-statistics-for/9781492072935/) and [Statistics and Probability on Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability)

- **Python**: I will do a tutorial on [PyFlo](https://pyflo.net/) with [Python3 reference](https://docs.python.org/3/reference/). With years surviving with C/C++, I guess Python won't be so hard with me. Still, I can't believe there is one day I jump into this disgusting language.

- **Data Structures**: I believe DSA art is in a lot of practicing, so here I come, I will practice [Data Structures on Hackerrank](https://www.hackerrank.com/domains/data-structures) and fun problem solving with [codewar](https://www.codewars.com/dashboard). I expect myself to solve at least a 6kyu problem on codewar everyday.
